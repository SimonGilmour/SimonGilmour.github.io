Mobile Beer App

A project in development during my 2nd year at Sask Polytechnic. This app will let you monitor the temperature of your beer (or beverage) using a bluetooth device.


Features

Sign On Capabilities

Want to keep track of your beverages? With this app you can sign in with Facebook or Google to ensure you always get your preferred temperature!

Custom notifications

Whether you want notification based on the current temperature or by time we've got you covered. With this app you can get up to date notifications to ensure your drink is at the perfect drinking temperature!

Custom temperatures

Whether you follow the experts or just want your drink chilled to your perferred tastes we've got you covered. This app will automatically save your preferred temperature of a particular beverage so you always get that ice cold beverage just the way you want.
